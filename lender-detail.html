Eddie Dabney, Jr.

Executive Vice President

Eddie is one of five sons of retired Master Sergeant Eddie and Teresia Dabney. A graduate of Lawton High School, he attended the banking programs at Great Plains Tech Center and the Graduate School of Banking in Madison, Wisconsin. Having established thousands of valued relationships, Eddie has 30 years of banking experience in Comanche County. Eddie has served on the boards of the Comanche County Rural Water District #4, Cache Economic Development Authority, Cache Chamber of Commerce, Lawton Northside Chamber of Commerce, the Oklahoma Bankers Association and is a Director at All America Bank®.

He enjoys exercising, cycling, fishing, golfing and assisting the local high school baseball program. Eddie has helped countless people accomplish their dreams throughout the past 18 years and is highly-experienced in cattle, real estate, construction, auto and commercial lending
