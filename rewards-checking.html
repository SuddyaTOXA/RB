  • Bank-to-bank transfers

  1. *We will refund up to $25 of foreign ATM fees each monthly statement cycle one lump sum.
  2. To receive rewards from Ultimate Rewards personal checking account, participants must:
    • Make 10 All America Bank VISA® debit card transactions per monthly statement cycle.
    • Receive your statements electronically.
    • Open your account with at least $50.
    • There is no minimum balance needed to maintain your accoun
  3. Earn 1.50% APY when you have up to $10,000 in your account. If you have more than $10,000 in your account, you’ll earn .50% APY. If you don’t meet the requirements outlined above, you’ll earn .25% APY. We will pay interest earned by your account at the end of each monthly billing cycle.
  4. This is a variable rate account and the interest rate can change. Fees could affect the earnings on this account.Learn more about our banking.
  5. Our monthly statement cycle ends on the 20th of each month. If the 20th falls on a Saturday, the cycle will end on Friday the 19th. If the 20th falls on a Sunday, the cycle will end on Monday the 21st. There are no rollover transactions.
  6. Qualifying VISA debit card sales transactions include each time you use your VISA debit card to make a purchase vendor or merchant. These transactions must post and clear your account during the payment cycle. ATM and ACH transactions do not qualify.
  7. Annual Percentage Yield (APY) is accurate as of 6/1/2016.
  8. There is a limit of two Ultimate Rewards Checking Accounts allowed per individual.
  9. Bank-to-Bank Transfer allows you to transfer money into or out of your All America Bank accounts from any other other bank, savings and loan, credit union, or brokerage account. This is a discretionary service. There is a $5,000 limit per day. Some restrictions apply. Please contact our customer service team to request to be set up for Bank-to-Bank Transfer.
